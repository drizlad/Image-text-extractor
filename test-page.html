🔍 Image Text Extractor - Test Page

How to Test:

  1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. The page will dim and your cursor will change to a crosshair
  3. Click and drag to select any text area below
  4. Release to capture and extract the text
  5. Review the extracted text in the popup
  6. Click "Copy Text" to copy to clipboard
